Indie is a 5 month old cockapoo puppy named after the archaeologist and explorer Indiana Jones (technically named after his dog who is also called Indiana). He enjoys eating socks, exploring and digging, but not for any crystal skulls or the ark of the covenant! He’s more likely to chase pebbles than run away from boulders and he’s too fussy to ever consider monkey brains however he’s still one of the best Indies out there.
